YCG_Nav_fan420 Album Review

10/10 This album is incredible! I don't undertand how they managed to combine so many styles and sounds into a single project. That second verse on Crackonomics might be one of my favorites of all time. Whoever wrote that is a genius. Can't wait for the next project!

YCG_Nav_fan420 Song Discussion

Today I would like to talk about the song 'YCG', the intro track off of No 9-5. It is most definitely a strong introduction to the artist and as soon as the beat comes in the song does not let up until it ends. Sonically, the beat was very minimalistic but allows Nav to show off his lyricall prowess and creativity, which he excells at. What do you guys think?
